Trips to Oktoberfest

Travel to Oktoberfest 2021 with Club Magellano!

Club Magellano is the international tour operator specializing in trips to the Oktoberfest operating since 2006 and which in 2014 brought over 7,000 people to Munich over the 3 weekends of celebration with bus trips from all over Italy and all over Europe. Club Magellano offers very varied travel packages , designed to best satisfy every need; there are offers designed specifically for the more adventurous, who do not want to give up partying and having fun despite having limited time and budget, such as the ONLY TRAVEL package, with direct arrival at the party on Saturday morning and departure on Saturday evening after the warehouses have closed, at classic offers with overnight stay, in which a visit to the city of Munich or other particular locations can be added. Club Magellano also offers the best price guarantee! If you find the same trip to oktoberfest 2021 from your city at a lower price, WE WILL REFUND THE DIFFERENCE . The travel solutions proposed by us depart on Friday evening, after working hours, and are designed to accompany you directly to the party, without having to take other means of transport, in good time to access the hangars without necessarily having to book for the 'entrance!
At the end of the day, our coaches will pick up passengers at the same point where they will be left in the morning, conveniently near the party, to take them to the hotel or, for those who have chosen solutions without overnight stays, directly to their cities of origin.
In each bus there is a guide who, as our manager, will remain available throughout the weekend for any type of need.
