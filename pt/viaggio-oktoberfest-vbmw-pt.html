Most Bavarian Oktoberfest Travel Package

For lovers of Bavaria who want to follow up on a whole Saturday spent at the oktoberfest, even a Sunday full of fun, relaxation...and even beer. In fact, this package includes, after Sunday breakfast, a suggestive trip to a typical Bavarian village, one of our exclusives. In fact here, between plains and hills, paths and waterways, you will immerse yourself in the sun of Bavaria, here as beautiful as ever, at the foot of an ancient monastery still inhabited by monks since 1300, in which it will be possible, for those who wish, taste the best Bavarian beer menu, and try the beer that the monks, keeping the same pure recipe for centuries, produce and sell. The BEST PRICE GUARANTEED formula is active for this Package which guarantees that neither in the agency nor on the web there is a lower price for this package. Trip description to the Oktoberfest – Travel Package + Hotel + Typical Village

The trip to the Oktoberfest for the "Travel + Hotel + Typical Village" package is certainly the most Bavarian, above all for how it is structured:
The coaches leave on Friday evening from the different loading points, each coach has a sign with the inscription CLUB MAGELLANO and is numbered; the sign with the number is displayed on the front windscreen of the coach and you will see and recognize it even from afar.

By the day of departure, those who have made the reservation will receive via e-mail and SMS on their mobile phone number (indicated at the time of booking), both the number of their coach, and the name and direct mobile number of our Tour Leader who will be present on the bus, who will have the function of assistant and coordinator, available for every need of the group and of individuals.

The arrival in Munich is expected before the party starts (09.00), compatibly with traffic and weather, in the immediate vicinity of the Theresienwiese (where the Oktoberfest takes place). Upon arrival, the group is clearly autonomous and free to decide how best to spend their day.Our Tour Leader is available, for those who wish, for any information or practical support.

The last beers inside the sheds are served at 10.30 pm, there is still half an hour left to finish them, and at 11.00 pm the sheds close their doors.

At 23.30 (you still have half an hour) the coach leaves to return to the hotel. Upon arrival at the hotel, the escort will check you in by handing you the keys to your room and will give you an appointment for the following morning.

After the typical German breakfast included in the price, the coach will head towards the typical Bavarian village, where you can spend pleasant hours of fun and relaxation, where you can immerse yourself, even more than in other travel packages, in the rural Bavarian atmosphere, surrounded by a breathtaking landscape. It will be possible for those who wish, to live a unique experience having lunch on the terrace of an ancient monastery, in which to taste the beer that the monks themselves produce, following the same original tradition for centuries, and tasting the typical dishes of the local culinary tradition. A great way to spend lunch with friends and make new friends.

In the early afternoon meeting and departure by bus for Italy. Arrival in Italy in the evening, compatibly with the respective cities of departure.
