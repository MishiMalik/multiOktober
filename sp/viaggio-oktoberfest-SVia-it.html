Oktoberfest Trip Description – Trip Only Package

The journey to the Oktoberfest for the "Travel Only" package is structured as follows: On Friday evening the coaches depart from the different loading locations. Each coach has a sign on the front windscreen with the writing CLUB MAGELLANO and is numbered; you will see and recognize it even from afar.
By the day of departure, those who have made the reservation will receive via email and SMS on their mobile phone number (indicated at the time of booking) both the number of their coach and the name and direct mobile number of our Tour Leader who will be present on the bus, available for every need of the group and of individuals and who will have the function of assistant and coordinator.
The arrival in Munich is expected, compatibly with traffic and weather, before the start of the party (09.00) in the immediate vicinity of the Theresienwiese, the place where the Oktoberfest takes place.After arrival the group is clearly autonomous and free to decide how best to spend their day. Our Tour Leader is available, for those who wish, for any information or practical support.
The last beers inside the sheds are served at 10.30pm;after half an hour to finish them, at 11.00 pm the sheds close their doors.
At 11.30 pm (after another half hour) the bus leaves to return to Italy.There you will find your guide waiting for you.
The arrival in Italy is scheduled for the early morning, taking into account the respective cities of departure.
Everything is organized to make your trip to the Oktoberfest the best ever.
