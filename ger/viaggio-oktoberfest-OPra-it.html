Travel package to the traditional Oktoberfest

Trip to the Oktoberfest for true lovers of the Bavarian tradition , who want to immerse themselves in the folklore of the Wiesn. This package includes lunch on Sunday in a typical Bavarian brewery , where you can taste the beer produced there . The price includes food and beer, to enjoy your meal without worries! For this package the BEST PRICE GUARANTEED formula is active which guarantees that there is no lower price for this package either in the agency or on the web. And if you find a better price, we'll refund the difference!!!

Travel program

  • Departure of the bus on Friday evening;

  • Arrive at Oktoberfest in the morning before the party opens.

  • Day at Oktoberfest, Munich's beer festival famous all over the world for its sheds, where millions of people take their seats every year, for the quality of the beer served and for the festive atmosphere that reigns;

  • At the end of the Festzelt (11.00 pm), meeting at the bus parking and return to the hotel;

  • Sunday morning German breakfast at the hotel;

  • Typical Bavarian lunch in the typical Bavarian Monastery Brewery where you can taste the excellent local cuisine while sipping a mug of beer produced here by Benedictine monks;

  • Departure around mid-afternoon;

  • Arrival at destination Sunday evening.

Oktoberfest Trip Description – Oktoberfest Package + Lunch

The trip to the Oktoberfest for the "Oktoberfest + Lunch" package is designed to discover the best of the Bavarian tradition: On Friday evening the coaches leave from the different loading points. Each coach has a sign on the front windscreen with the writing CLUB MAGELLANO and is numbered;
you will see and recognize it even from afar. By the day of departure, those who have made the reservation will receive via email and SMS on their mobile phone number (indicated at the time of booking) both the number of their coach and the name and direct mobile number of our Tour Leader who will be present on the bus, available for every need of the group and of individuals and who will have the function of assistant and coordinator.
The arrival in Munich is expected, compatibly with traffic and weather, before the start of the party (09.00) in the immediate vicinity of the Theresienwiese, the place where the Oktoberfest takes place. After arrival the group is clearly autonomous and free to decide how best to spend their day. Our Tour Leader is available, for those who wish, for any information or practical support. The last beers inside the sheds are served at 10.30pm; after half an hour to finish them, at 11.00 pm the sheds close their doors. At 11.30pm (after another half hour) the coach leaves to return to the hotel. The escort will check you in on your arrival at the hotel, handing over the keys to your room. Dopo una colazione tipicamente tedesca inclusa nel prezzo, il pullman si dirigerà verso la suggestiva birreria nella quale ci si fermerà per immergersi nuovamente nel festoso clima bavarese, degustando la birra artigianale qui prodotta e l’ottima cucina. Il modo perfetto per passare il pranzo in compagnia e fare nuove amicizie. Nel primo pomeriggio ritrovo e partenza in bus per l’Italia.
L’arrivo in Italia è previsto in serata, tenendo conto delle rispettive città di partenza. Questo pacchetto è organizzato per rendere il vostro viaggio all’Oktoberfest il più “bavarese” possibile.
